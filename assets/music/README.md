# Music Requirements

## Background Music
- Add 1-3 royalty-free music tracks to this folder
- Duration: 60+ seconds (will be looped/trimmed as needed)
- Tone: Calm, dramatic, mystical
- Format: .mp3

## Recommended Sources:
- YouTube Audio Library
- Freesound.org
- Zapsplat
- Artlist (paid)

## Settings:
- The music will play at 15% volume (configurable in main.py)
- Mixed with voiceover automatically

## Sample filenames:
- mystical_ambient_01.mp3
- epic_cinematic_02.mp3
